FOOD4THOTH Philosophy
FOOD4THOTH is devoted to a quick, easy, and free guide to diving into the world of Tarot. Our goal is to make Tarot more accessible to everyone. Tarot has a stigma of being associate with fortune telling and performing magic which unfortunately turns a lot of people away. We teach Tarot by way of storytelling and providing insight into life’s questions. What you do with the answers received from Tarot are up to you. We are creatures of free will, and there’s not a single entity who holds the keys to our future except us.
Tarot can be as simple or as complex as you want it to be. With countless ways to interact with the cards, FOOD4THOTH Tarot barely scratches the surface. There’s many experts who have written books and host various sites that can provide you with further practice. However, FOOD4THOTH Tarot provides the information in a simple and easy way to get you started!
