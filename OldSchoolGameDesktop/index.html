Welcome to the Terminal Game

Move using the Arrow keys, W/A/S/D keys, or touch/click the screen.

Avoid the red squares of doom and collect the green squares of treasure.

Collect power-ups for special abilities!

Power-ups:

  • Orange squares: Speed Boost
  • Blue squares: Invincibility
  • Purple squares: Score Multiplier

Every 10 points advances you to a new level with new challenges.
